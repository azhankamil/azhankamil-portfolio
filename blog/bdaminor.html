Minor in Big Data Analytics

Minor in Big Data Analytics

  • Tan Li Tung
  • Blog

Big Data Analytics has been a hot topics these few years. There are lots of online courses and resources available online. But there is still a problem, where to start? Well looking up opportunities in your own university might be a good way! In this post, I am going to share some information and my experiences about Minor in Big Data Analytics offered in Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS (UTP).

In UTP, you are allowed to take this minor when you enter your undergraduate studies and this minor is offered only for Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EE), Petroleum Geo-science (PG), Petroleum Engineering (PE) and Mechanical Engineering (ME) structure E students. There will be 5 subjects under this minor and you need to complete all the subjects to get your minor certificate!

Project That I Did For Artificial Intelligence

In my study, I managed to complete a machine learning to predict the concrete strength given the amount of each material. The project is done using R, and the full code is on my GitHub!

In this upcoming semester, I will be doing a project on Data Mining! Once completed, I will upload to my Github and also my website! Do not forget to check it out!

Is Taking Minor in Big Data Analytics Worthy?

Pros:

  • You will learn more skills than others.
  • Your academic transcript will be have more value if you manage to understand all the subjects.
  • You will learn to manage your time efficiently.
  • You will get to know more friends.
  • You will have multiple projects to showcase in your portfolio!

Cons:

  • If you did not manage your time well, minor studies can be a burden to you.
  • If you get bad grades in minor, it will affect your CGPA.

As we can see, taking a minor subject might help you or burden you. It all depends on you ability to cope with your major subjects and activities that you join in the university.

However, the university will not simply allow you to take a minor. he academic department will check your results in your major study, and ask for your study plan if you are eligible for taking a minor!

How to Register?

To register, you will need to contact the academic executive of Fundamental and Applied Science Department (FASD) to ensure that the subjects are offered during the particular semester (sometimes they do not offer due to too few students taking the subject). If the subject is offered, you will need to go to registry to register for the subject in your student portal.

The only way to finish is to START.
